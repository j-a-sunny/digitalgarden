[Supply chain management (SCM)](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/s/scm.asp) is the strategic coordination of the entire product lifecycle, from raw material sourcing and manufacturing to final delivery and returns. Key goals include reducing costs, improving efficiency, increasing velocity, and enhancing customer satisfaction. It involves five core pillars: planning, sourcing, manufacturing, delivery, and returns.

**Key Components and Functions**

- **Core Pillars:** The process involves planning demand, sourcing materials, production, delivery, and managing returns (reverse logistics).
- **Key Activities:** Involves warehousing, inventory management, transportation, and logistics network design.
- **Objectives:** Aim to improve inventory visibility, boost efficiency, enhance product quality, and increase flexibility.
- **Technologies:** Tools like [SAP SCM](https://nav-it.com/sap-supply-chain-management-the-ultimate-guide/) are used to manage the flow of goods, data, and finances. 

**Benefits and Strategies**

- **Cost Reduction:** SCM helps identify inefficiencies and reduce excess costs.
- **Collaboration:** Enhances trust and communication among partners.
- **Agility:** Enables businesses to adapt to market changes.
- **Key Roles:** Includes positions like Chief Supply Chain Officer (CSCO), Logistics Managers, and Procurement Managers. 

**Top SCM System Examples:**

- **SAP S/4HANA:** Provides end-to-end visibility, demand planning, and manufacturing execution.
- **Oracle SCM Cloud:** A flexible, cloud-based platform covering procurement, logistics, and manufacturing.
- **Manhattan Associates:** A top choice for warehouse management, transportation, and omnichannel retail.
- **Blue Yonder:** Specializes in AI-driven supply chain planning and logistics.
- **Infor CloudSuite:** Offers industry-specific solutions for manufacturing and distribution.
- **Plex Systems:** A cloud-based solution for shop floor automation, widely used in automotive and manufacturing.
- **Microsoft Dynamics 365:** A versatile ERP with strong supply chain, warehousing, and transportation modules.
- **E2open:** A network-based platform designed to link supply chain partners and improve visibility.
- **o9 Solutions:** Uses "digital twin" technology for AI-powered demand forecasting and planning.
- **Kinaxis RapidResponse:** Focuses on concurrent planning and rapid decision-making in supply chains. 

**Key SCM Functionalities:**

- **Planning:** Demand forecasting, supply planning, and Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP).
- **Procurement:** Sourcing raw materials and supplier management.
- **Manufacturing:** Production scheduling and execution.
- **Logistics:** Transportation management, warehousing, and inventory tracking.
